Tags can have attributes and be styled.

"a" stands for anchor. href is an attribute that stores a url
"br" breaks lines. "b" and "strong" both bold text. "i" and "em" will italicize text.
"u" makes things underlined.

"img" is the image tag and "src" is the source attribute.
